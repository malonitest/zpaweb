### 2.4 Content Guidelines for AI Optimization

#### Writing for AI Search Engines

**1. Natural Language & Conversational Tone**
- Write like you're answering a friend's question
- Use "you" and "your" frequently
- Address questions directly
- Example: "Wondering how fast you can get money? Here's the exact timeline..."

**2. Question-Answer Format**
- Start sections with questions
- Provide clear, direct answers first
- Then expand with details
- Example format:
  ```
  ## How Long Does Approval Take?
  
  **Short answer:** 15 minutes or less.
  
  **Detailed explanation:** Once you submit your online application...
  ```

**3. Structured Content**
- Use bullet points and numbered lists
- Add comparison tables
- Include step-by-step guides
- Use clear headings (H2, H3)

**4. Semantic Richness**
- Include related terms naturally
- Cover topic comprehensively
- Link related concepts
- Use synonyms and variations

**5. Data & Specifics**
- Include specific numbers
- Add dates and timelines
- Provide concrete examples
- Show calculations and formulas

**6. Update Frequency**
- Date stamp all content
- Update yearly (minimum)
- Add "Last updated" notices
- Keep statistics current
